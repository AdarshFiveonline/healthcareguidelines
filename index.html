health, safety & environment guidelines

  • Always carry visitor's pass/employee id card all the time.
  • Visitors are not allowed in AMNT without valid entry pass.
  • Please declare the belongings like laptop, pendrive, camera, IT tools at the gate.
  • Over speeding, Overtaking are strictly prohibited in campus premises.
  • Follow speed limit signage's strictly.
  • Photography is not permitted inside the premises.
  • Carrying/Consumption of any form of Drug/Alcohol is strictly prohibited inside the campus premises.
  • Stop & report all unsafe act & unsafe condition to your respective floor leader.
  • Please don't throw litters on floor and common area other than designated place.
  • Please take assistance of receptionist at ground floor for any support and guidance.
  • Follow safety signage's installed across campus premises.
  • Please get familiarised with Emergency evacuation route & allotted assembly points once.
  • In case of any emergency, don't get panic and strictly follow your Floor leader instructions.
  • In case of Fire please do not use lift, use nearest staircase.
  • Medical facility is available on Ground floor.
  • Emergency First aid kit is available in break out area of your respective floor.
  • Please take proper work permit from concern Team before commencing any activity.
  • In case of any emergency, if you are alone connect directly to Helpdesk No.:- 5800/5542 and don't forget to mention your name, location & any injuries if occurred.
  • Ensure that you are always accompanied by your host during the visit.
  • Always remember Emergency No.:- 5800/5542, Main Security No.:- 5700/5701 and for Firstaid:- 5959.
